Hi, I'm Patience

Born in Africa Nigeria, I didn't experience much of technology. My world was outside, playing with my friends, doing my chore, and helping my mom when it was time to make our meals, they all happened outside. When I came to the U.S at the age of 11, I was mind blown by the world, to me, a new world filled with so many interesting things, and one of them was technology. After a while, I realized that technology had a world of its own and affects everyone in the world. I wanted to be a part of that world and use it to do good things in the world.

I am now a graduate of Taylor University with a degree in computer science-digital media and systems. I choose this major because of its vast range of studies from computer science to business, design, and more. I hope to utilize what I have learned and also continue to grow with new opportunities that may present themselves along the way.

I enjoy watching Korean dramas, and anime. I also love drinking tea, spending time with my younger siblings, and having conversations with others. I would love to travel outside of the U.S and visit places like Japan, Korea, and London. A fun fact about me is that I feel incredible comfort and calm during rainy/stormy weather.
